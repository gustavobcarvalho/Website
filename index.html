The Legendary Crystals - WarShattered

Game Genre : Action RPG, isometric, hack and slash with loot and progression - Fully functional ARPG mechanics, basic crafting, and character creation and progression
History: After the last great war, the world was shattered and consumed by legendary crystals, sources of immense power and destruction. Only the Dustborn — the player — can wield them without falling into corruption. The prophecy speaks: “The last Dustborn will bring peace and balance to the world, sealing the portals of the Deathlands.” But the Deathlands are older than war. In forgotten ages, when battle tore the veil between worlds, they were first opened — and silence there has never meant absence, but vigilance. Within a crystal sealed by ancient pacts, something lay dormant. Not in sleep, but in waiting. And when time could no longer contain what dwelled within, the crystal shattered — not by force, but by calling. From the fractures of oblivion, you emerge. The vestments you wear belong to an era when the Order walked between light and shadow, keepers of truths the world no longer dares to speak. You carry memories never given, a silence not your own — yet the Deathlands remember. Your path is to traverse broken realms, gather the Legendary Crystals, and uncover what was sealed — and what now waits, patient, at the edge of a war that never truly ended.
Current Status: Playable demo featuring core combat, loot, talent trees, and three main classes

Power Champions - Chaos Paradise

Game Genre: Action-Adventure RPG, hack and slash with attribute progression, cards, and ascension
History: In the beginning, there was only darkness and purple lights, until a vortex absorbed them. From this event, Ellora was born — the goddess who created Zarion, a planet of abundant nature and sacred places, and later shaped other worlds, among them Zeloth, marked by technology. Yet from Zeloth’s essence emerged evil, spreading corruption and destruction until it reached Zarion. From this shadow came KillDeath, the great villain who conquered planets and unleashed chaos across the universe. Hope now lies with the Power Champions, the chosen heroes destined to protect Zarion and face the forces threatening to consume all creation.
Current Status: Released on Steam, featuring fast-paced combat against hordes of enemies, progression based on cards and attributes (damage, defense, and speed), ascension after completing the main journey, farming maps with hidden treasures, and unlockable skins through achievements.

Nelson's Hat

Game Genre: 2D side-scrolling platformer, jump’n’run with magical hat abilities, obstacles, and enemies
History: You play as Nelson, a hero who owns magical hats — each granting unique powers. With them, Nelson must overcome obstacles, defeat alien creatures, and progress through challenging levels. The world blends fantasy and sci-fi elements, focusing on platforming and skillful use of hats to advance.
Current Status: A demo is available on itch.io. The game features classic side-scrolling action with precise jumps, hat-based abilities, enemy encounters, and a lighthearted, fun platforming experience.
