N2JG is an independent game development studio based in Brazil, built to turn bold ideas into unforgettable interactive experiences. Our mission is to explore new ways of storytelling, challenge traditional mechanics, and create worlds that inspire players of all ages.

At N2JG, we believe games are more than entertainment — they’re bridges between people, cultures, and emotions. Every project begins with curiosity, grows through innovation, and comes to life with dedication. Whether it’s epic adventures, clever puzzles, or deep narratives, we strive to surprise and connect.

What drives us: creativity, authenticity, and a passion for games

What we do: game development for PC, mobile, and consoles
